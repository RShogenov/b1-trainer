Validated Feature Gaps (Not Solved by Any Tool)
Flat-rate pricing
ZenMaid's per-cleaner model means 5-person teams pay $109+/mo. Nobody offers flat-rate for unlimited cleaners under $100.
Mobile-first crew app
ZenMaid Google Play: 3.2/5. Crews use phones, not desktops. No tool has a genuinely good crew-facing mobile experience.
Bilingual support
Cleaning crews are predominantly Spanish-speaking. ZenMaid has no Spanish language support. No competitor addresses this.
Standalone time clock
ZenMaid only allows clock-in on published appointments. Cleaners can't clock in/out independently. No clean payroll data output.
The Product Opportunity
ZenMaid captured 1,000 customers in 11 years at $216/mo ARPU — that's $2.6M ARR from 0.28% of the market. A flat-rate ($49–79/mo, unlimited cleaners) mobile-first alternative with a 4.5+ rated crew app in English and Spanish, standalone time clock, and basic payroll export would have a structural pricing advantage and address the top feature gaps simultaneously. Scheduling mistakes cost operators $10,000–21,000/year — the product pays for itself in prevented double-bookings in the first month. The category is proven, the TAM is massive, and the leader has barely scratched the surface.
